Alex Leitch

Hi there. I am a software developer and creative technologist in Toronto, Canada. I'm one of the founders of Site 3 coLaboratory, a community makerspace with an emphasis on industrial arts, and a founding director of DMG.to, a community group that helps women learn to build video games.

In my spare time, I do a lot of public speaking, and I build amazing things: large installations involving fire, small video games involving bicycles, a palm-sized server that will populate any room with phone-controlled, linked web applications. You know. Art. Interested in building that?
