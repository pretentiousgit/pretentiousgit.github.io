Alex Leitch

Hi there. I am a software developer and creative technologist living and working in Toronto, Canada. I'm one of the founders of Site 3 coLaboratory, a community makerspace with an emphasis on industrial arts, and a founding director of DMG.to, a community group that helps women learn to build video games.

I'm currently with Heist, where I am lead developer on Field Guide, an app to return better information from user experience interviews. I've also worked for SickKids Hospital, OCADu, and The Art Gallery of Ontario.

In my spare time, I build adventures with Team AdventureBox, do public speaking on a variety of topics, and spend some time on Twitter.
